If you are a consumer in the EU/EEA, you have the right to withdraw from your purchase within 14 days without giving any reason. However, because QuantraVision is digital content delivered immediately upon purchase, you expressly acknowledge and consent to:

1. Immediate access to the App upon purchase
2. Waiver of the 14-day withdrawal right by commencing use
3. Right to request refund through Google Play within the first 48 hours under Google's refund policy

**To exercise withdrawal rights (if applicable):**
Contact Google Play Support within 14 days of purchase and request a refund citing EU Consumer Rights Directive.

### GDPR Compliance

**Regulation (EU) 2016/679 - General Data Protection Regulation:**

QuantraVision is 100% GDPR-compliant because:
1. ✅ **No personal data is collected** - All processing occurs on-device
2. ✅ **No data is transmitted** to servers or third parties
3. ✅ **No cookies or tracking** - App does not use web technologies
4. ✅ **No profiling** - No behavioral analysis or user tracking
5. ✅ **Data minimization** - Only Google Play transaction data (managed by Google)

**Your GDPR Rights:**
- Right to Access: We hold no data to access
- Right to Erasure: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- Right to Portability: Not applicable (no data collected)
- Right to Object: Not applicable (no processing of personal data)

**Data Controller:** Google LLC (for purchase data only)  
**Our Role:** We are NOT a data controller or processor

**Markets in Financial Instruments Directive II:**

QuantraVision is NOT:

- Subject to MiFID II investment firm regulations

**We do NOT:**
