Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 23, 2025

QuantraVision™ by Lamont Labs operates entirely on your device. It does not transmit, share, or collect any personal or financial data.

1. Data Collection

  • No account, login, or remote storage is used.
  • All detection logs remain in local encrypted storage.
  • No analytics, crash reports, or telemetry are sent without explicit consent.

2. Permissions

Overlay permission is required solely to render visual highlights. Storage permission is required to save exports. Battery optimization exemption is optional to ensure continuous overlay operation.

3. Purchases

Billing transactions occur through Google Play Billing. No payment information is stored or accessible by Lamont Labs.

4. Children

QuantraVision is designed for adult users (18+) engaged in financial education or simulation only.

5. Contact

Questions may be directed to Lamont Labs via Lamontlabs@proton.me.
